# 🎮 Tetrark Studios

> We build what we believe in — not what the market expects.

---

## 🧭 Who We Are

Tetrark Studios is an independent game development studio driven by vision, not trends.

We create games because we have something to say — not because something needs to sell.

Every project we work on is built with intention: from the smallest mechanic to the largest narrative arc.

---

## 🎯 Our Philosophy

We reject the idea that games are just products.

To us, games are:
- 🎮 Experiences to be lived  
- 🧠 Ideas to be explored  
- 🌌 Worlds to be discovered  

We value:
- Creative integrity over profitability  
- Depth over accessibility shortcuts  
- Identity over mass appeal  

If a choice must be made, we choose the vision.

## 🛠️ How We Build

We focus on creating systems that matter.

- Mechanics are designed to interact, not exist in isolation  
- Worlds react to player presence  
- Stories emerge through action, not exposition  

We don't aim to guide the player.
We aim to immerse them.

---

## 🧪 Development Approach

- Small team, high ownership  
- Iterative and experimental workflows  
- No unnecessary bureaucracy  
- Decisions driven by design, not metrics
